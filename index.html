Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelago located in Southeast Asia, consisting of over 7,000 islands. Known for its stunning beaches, rich biodiversity, and vibrant culture, the country boasts a mix of indigenous and colonial influences, reflected in its festivals, cuisine, and languages. The capital, Manila, is a bustling metropolis, while destinations like Palawan and Boracay lure tourists with their natural beauty. The Philippines is also home to friendly and hospitable people, making it a popular travel destination with a growing economy driven by agriculture, tourism, and remittances from overseas workers.
